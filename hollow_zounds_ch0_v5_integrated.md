# HOLLOW ZOUNDS

### Final Script v5.0 — INTEGRATED LAYOUT (No White Text Breaks)

---

## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

This chapter should flow as ONE continuous vertical image.
No white page breaks. No alternating text/art.
Every panel has art. Text sits ON the art as narration boxes,
caption boxes, or floating text — never on a blank white page.

Think: traditional manhwa. Text breathes inside the artwork.
The reader never leaves the visual world.

---

## CURSOR STYLE DIRECTIONS (apply to every panel)

**Narration boxes:**
- Background: rgba(0,0,0,0.75) — semi transparent dark
- Left border: 3px solid #8B00FF (electric purple)
- Text color: #FFFFFF white
- Font: Bangers (Google Fonts)
- Font size: 36px
- Padding: 12px 20px
- Border radius: 0 (sharp corners)
- Max width: 85% of panel width
- Position: top-left or bottom-left depending on panel
- Stack vertically with 6px gap between boxes

**Floating narration (Hollow's voice — more casual lines):**
- No box, no border
- Font: Bangers
- Font size: 42px
- Color: white #FFFFFF
- Subtle text shadow: 2px 2px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.9)
- Used for punchlines and one-liners that need room to breathe

**Chapter intro text (Panel 0):**
- Full overlay on dark atmospheric art
- Font: Bangers
- Large centered text, white
- Key words slightly larger for emphasis

**Panel gaps:**
- 0px between panels — seamless vertical flow
- Each panel bleeds into the next
