## BEAT 1 — COLD OPEN: THE GATE
*Panels 1–3 | Mood: Dark, cinematic, ominous. Dead serious. This looks like Solo Leveling.*

🔊 *City ambience. Distant traffic. A dog barking. Normal night.*

---

**PANEL 1 — FULL**
*Camera: Low angle looking up. Night sky over a city block. A massive crack of purple-black energy tears open in the air above a 7-Eleven. The gate pulses — ominous, enormous, wrong. Debris floats upward. A parked car alarm goes off.*

**SFX: KKRRRAAAACK**

🔊 *Car alarm. Then — all city noise cuts out. Dead silence except for the gate's low hum.*

[ Somewhere in the city. 11:47 PM. ]
[ A Class-D Gate. Unexpected. Unregistered. ]
[ Population within blast radius: 34. ]
[ Hunters in the area: 3. ]

---

**PANEL 2 — HALF**
*Camera: Street level. Three hunters in full gear — armored, ranked, professional. Hunter badges visible. They look at the gate with trained focus. Behind them, slightly out of frame, a figure in joggers and an anime tee holding a plastic 7-Eleven bag.*

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(serious, hand on weapon)*:
"D-rank gate. Unregistered. We move in and clear before it expands."

**HUNTER B:**
"On your call."

**HUNTER C** *(noticing the figure behind them)*:
"Hey kid — GET BACK."

---

**PANEL 3 — THIRD**
*Camera: Close on Hollow from behind. Locs with purple streaks. Cheap practice sword on his back. 7-Eleven bag in hand. Posture completely relaxed. Not scared. Not excited. Just... looking.*

*[ Hm. ]*
*[ That's crazy. ]*

## BEAT 2 — PULLED IN
*Panels 4–5 | Mood: Chaos. Fast. Disorienting.*

🔊 *The gate's hum rises to a ROAR.*

---

**PANEL 4 — FULL**
*Camera: Wide. The gate SURGES — a shockwave of black energy explodes outward. The three hunters brace themselves. Hollow is mid-reach into his bag pulling out a sandwich.*

**SFX: BWOOOOOOM**

**HUNTER A (DARIUS):** "IT'S EXPANDING — EVERYONE RU—"

---

**PANEL 5 — FULL**
*Camera: The gate inhales everything like a vacuum. All four figures yanked inward. The hunters are screaming. Hollow's face is that of a man who just dropped his phone in the toilet. Mildly inconvenienced.*

**SFX: SHHHHHHHHK**

**HOLLOW** *(getting pulled in, deadpan)*:
"Bro."

[ Population remaining outside gate: 33. ]
[ Hunters pulled inside: 3. ]
[ Civilians pulled inside: 1. ]
[ Civilian rank: E. ]
[ Civilian luck stat: ///ERROR/// ]

## BEAT 3 — INSIDE THE GATE
*Panels 6–9 | Mood: Terrifying environment. Hollow is not getting the memo.*

🔊 *Inside: total silence at first. Then — low growling. Everywhere. From the dark.*

---

**PANEL 6 — FULL**
*Camera: Wide establishing shot. Inside the gate — ruined dungeon, crumbling stone, dark crackling sky, packs of shadow wolves prowling in the distance. Beautiful and horrifying.*

[ Inside a Class-D Gate. ]
[ Estimated monster count: 40+ ]
[ Average hunter survival rate for solo E-rank entry: 0% ]

---

**PANEL 7 — HALF**
*Camera: The three hunters land hard, skills activating, weapons out. They look terrified. Hollow lands in an accidentally clean crouch and checks his sandwich first.*

**HUNTER B** *(scanning frantically)*:
"Forty-plus signatures. Ambush nest. We need to regroup and—"

**HOLLOW** *(examining sandwich)*:
"It's good. Barely squished."

**HUNTER C** *(staring)*:
"...WHAT IS WRONG WITH YOU."

---

**PANEL 8 — HALF**
*Camera: Hunters huddled, weapons drawn, staring at Hollow.*

**HUNTER A (DARIUS):**
"Listen. Stay BEHIND us. Do NOT engage. Do NOT move. You will die."

**HOLLOW** *(nodding, unwrapping sandwich)*:
"Yeah yeah. I got you."

*[ I got absolutely nothing. ]*

---

**PANEL 9 — THIRD**
*Camera: Wide. In the distance — shadow wolves notice them. Eyes opening in the dark. Dozens.*

🔊 *Growling builds. Low. Layered. Like it's coming from the walls.*

**SFX: grrrrrrrrrr**

**HUNTER B** *(whispering)*:
"...They see us."

---

## BEAT 4 — THE FIGHT. THE COST.
*Panels 10–16 | Mood: Real danger. Real loss. Then — the accident.*

🔊 *Combat noise. Visceral. Ugly. No music — just impacts, snarls, screams.*

---

**PANEL 10 — FULL**
*Camera: Cinematic wide. Wolves CHARGE. The three hunters explode into action — real skills, real power, real professionals doing what they're trained to do. This looks incredible.*

**SFX: RAAAAHHHH**

[ The hunters were professionals. ]
[ Combined rank: B-. ]
[ They had cleared over 200 gates between them. ]

---

**PANEL 11 — FULL**
*Camera: The fight in full swing. Hunters holding the line. Beautiful brutal choreography. In the background — barely visible — Hollow is eating his sandwich and watching like it's a movie.*

🔊 *This is the loudest panel in the chapter. Everything is noise.*

---

**PANEL 12 — HALF**
*Camera: Hunter C mid-battle. A wolf catches him from the side — too fast, wrong angle. He goes down hard. The panel is quick, not lingered on, but the SOUND makes it real.*

**SFX: CRACK**

🔊 *Silence for exactly one beat after the crack. Just one.*

**HUNTER B** *(turning, horrified)*:
"C—!"

[ Hunter Jerome Ellis. Age 26. First year out of the academy. ]
[ He had a photo of his mom in his helmet. ]

---

**PANEL 13 — HALF**
*Camera: Hunter B freezes for half a second — that human half-second of grief in combat. It costs him. Two wolves take him from both sides simultaneously. He's fast enough to block one. Not both.*

**SFX: SHHK**

🔊 *A wet sound. Then silence again.*

*He doesn't fall dramatically. He just... stops.*

[ Hunter Marcus Webb. Age 29. Owed his buddy twenty dollars. ]
[ Never paid it back. ]
[ He always meant to. ]

---

**PANEL 14 — FULL**
*Camera: Wide. Darius alone now. Wolves circling. Exhausted, bleeding, back against a crumbling wall. He looks around for his team. Finds only Hollow — who has finished his sandwich and is now watching with mild interest.*

🔊 *The growling fills the silence. Darius's breathing. That's all.*

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(barely standing)*:
"...It's just us."

**HOLLOW:**
"Yeah I peeped."

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(staring at him)*:
"Are you SERIOUS right now—"

---

**PANEL 15 — FULL**
*Camera: The Alpha emerges. Twice the size of the others. Stepping out of the shadows slowly. This is a boss entrance.*

🔊 *Everything else goes quiet when the Alpha appears. Even the other wolves.*

**SFX: BOOOM — (footstep)**

[ Alpha-class shadow wolf. ]
[ One has not been recorded in a D-rank gate before. ]
[ This will be in the report. ]

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(quiet, to himself)*:
"...We didn't account for an Alpha."

---

**PANEL 16 — HALF**
*Camera: The Alpha raises its claw over Darius. Darius closes his eyes. This is it.*

[ Hunter Darius Lowe. Age 31. Three kids. ]
[ His last thought was: I should've called in sick. ]

**SFX: SHHHHK**

---

## BEAT 5 — THE INCIDENT
*Panels 17–21 | Mood: The stupid miracle.*

🔊 *The swing — then a different sound. Not impact. A slip. Stone crumbling.*

---

**PANEL 17 — HALF**
*Camera: The Alpha's claw misses by two inches. Its foot slipped on a loose stone. It stumbles forward, momentum carrying it headfirst into the wall.*

**SFX: BONK**

*The Alpha slides down the wall, unconscious.*

*Silence.*

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(eyes still closed)*:
"...I'm alive?"

---

**PANEL 18 — THIRD**
*Camera: Reveal. Hollow is behind where the Alpha fell. He tripped over a rock two seconds before the swing — and his stumbling knocked loose the exact stone the Alpha stepped on. He is rubbing his knee, mildly annoyed. He has no idea.*

**HOLLOW:**
"Ow. This stupid rock— yo you good?"

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(staring at unconscious Alpha, then at Hollow)*:
"...You just—"

**HOLLOW:**
"I stubbed my toe. I'm fine though."

🔊 *A beat of pure silence. Just Darius processing.*

---

**PANEL 19 — FULL**
*Camera: Wide. The remaining wolves, seeing their Alpha fall, go FERAL and charge directly at Hollow. Darius screams.*

**HUNTER A (DARIUS):**
"RUUUUN—"

---

**PANEL 20 — FULL**
*Camera: Hollow turns to run. His cheap practice sword snags a rock and flies off his back, spinning into the air. Slow motion. The sword catches the dungeon light. Genuinely, accidentally beautiful.*

**SFX: SWIIIING**

🔊 *For just this panel — silence. The beauty of the accidental.*

*[ Not how I wanted that to go. ]*

---

**PANEL 21 — FULL**
*Camera: The spinning sword clips a crumbling pillar. Chain reaction. Architecture collapses in sequence — BOOM, BOOM, BOOM, BOOM. Stone slabs take out wolf after wolf. A massive dust cloud.*

**SFX: BOOM. BOOM. BOOM. BOOM.**

🔊 *And then — silence again. Ringing silence. The kind after something very loud.*

*The wolves are buried.*

---

**PANEL 22 — WIDE**
*Camera: Cinematic. Dust settling. Hollow standing in the wreckage — dusty, one shoe untied, empty sandwich wrapper in hand. Darius beside him, helmet cracked, completely speechless.*

🔊 *City noise bleeds back in from nowhere. Distant traffic. Like the world forgot to stay dramatic.*

**HOLLOW** *(looking at the destruction)*:
"...Hm."

**HOLLOW** *(looking at where his sword landed, crushed under rubble)*:
"Sword kinda mid anyway."

## BEAT 7 — EXIT. THE WORLD FINDS OUT.
*Panels 28–33 | Mood: Back to comedy. Then a slow burn ending.*

🔊 *Gate exit: immediate sensory overload. City noise, sirens, voices, cameras. The world rushing back in.*

---

**PANEL 28 — HALF**
*Camera: The gate exit. Darius stumbling out, leaning on Hollow. Registration agents and emergency response waiting. Cameras. Flashing lights. Chaos.*

**REGISTRATION AGENT** *(clipboard, scanning)*:
"...Three registered B-rank hunters entered. We're reading four signatures came out?"

**HUNTER A (DARIUS)** *(exhausted)*:
"Two didn't make it. The fourth one's a civilian. E-rank."

**REGISTRATION AGENT:**
"He helped?"

**HUNTER A (DARIUS):**
"...I don't want to talk about it."

---

**PANEL 29 — THIRD**
*Camera: The agent scans Hollow. Scanner beeps. Glitches. Beeps again.*

**SCANNER READOUT:**
`NAME: HOLLOW ZOUNDS`
`RANK: E`
`LUCK STAT: ///ERROR///`

**REGISTRATION AGENT** *(staring at scanner, then at Hollow)*:
"...Sir, is this device registered to you?"

**HOLLOW:**
"What?"

**REGISTRATION AGENT:**
"Your luck stat isn't displaying. The scanner thinks it's broken."

**HOLLOW** *(nodding, very serious)*:
"Yeah. That's normal."

---

**PANEL 30 — THIRD**
*Camera: Close on Darius's face in the background. Just his face.*

*He has seen two of his colleagues die tonight.*
*He has watched a teenager eat a sandwich through a dungeon.*
*He watched that same teenager accidentally defeat forty wolves and an Alpha.*
*He does not have the words.*

🔊 *Dead silence on this panel. Just his face.*

---

**PANEL 31 — FULL**
*Camera: Wide. Hollow walking away from the scene alone — police tape, emergency vehicles, the gate fading behind him. He looks tiny. But his chest is out.*

🔊 *City noise fully restored. Normal. Mundane. Indifferent.*

*[ Cleared my first gate. ]*
*[ Got my first level. ]*
*[ Put points in luck. ]*

---

**PANEL 32 — HALF**
*Camera: Close on his face. Sincere. Almost sweet.*

*[ Grandma's gonna be happy about the money. ]*
*[ And one day... ]*
*[ ...I'm gonna get lucky. ]*

---

**PANEL 33 — FULL — FINAL PANEL**
*Camera: Wide. Day breaks. Hollow rounds a corner and disappears. The city wakes up.*

*Cut to: screens. Phone. Laptop. Tablet. Hunter forums. News sites. His name. His stat sheet. That glitched scanner reading. Spreading.*

🔊 *The sound of notifications. One. Then five. Then too many to count.*

**FORUM POST 1:** *"E-rank civilian cleared a D-gate alongside B-ranks and WALKED OUT??"*
**FORUM POST 2:** *"his luck stat just says ERROR what is this"*
**FORUM POST 3:** *"HOLLOW ZOUNDS??? what kinda name is that"*
**FORUM POST 4:** *"he's either the most dangerous man alive or the luckiest idiot on earth"*
**FORUM POST 5:** *"...why does the scanner say he's still leveling??"*

*Black screen. One line.*

[ Somewhere across the city, twelve guild masters just got the same notification. ]

**END OF CHAPTER 0**
